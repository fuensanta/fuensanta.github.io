The Dreamer and The Doer

Tian Fang

Hello, my name is Tian Fang, I am from Shanghai, China.

If name one most unique aspect of my being, that would be my conflicting yet harmonized personality.

I love to make full-detailed plan for every day life (which I do) and I would also pack up a small luggage, leaving at 3 a.m after watching a documentary to go for an “adventure”.

I play melancholy or even desperate piano, like Tchaikovsky, or Billie Holiday, I also enjoy chanson, bossa nova, or heavy metal.

I am a huge fan for vintage stuff, anything, from lady’s handbags to cigarette cases, or pin-up posters, or old silver spoon with some old family’s badge (if I use the write word) on it; I also go for avan-garde shows and performance arts

Interestingly, my conflicting personality is tightly associated with and somewhat shaped by the city I am from. If anyone heard of or know of anything about Shanghai, he/she would understand what I am talking about. If anyone is interested in this topic, I would love to share my experience, just don’t expect me to stop once I start babbling.
